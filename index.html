PROFILE

Meet Your Nail Artist

Hiiii! There are so many new faces around here lately, so I thought I'd reintroduce myself. I'm Avery Dansie — a nail artist based in Salt Lake City, Utah. I specialize in structured gel and gel X nails

A little about me: I'm a huge people person who loves talking to everyone I cross paths with, I'm endlessly obsessed with Swig and soda in general, and outside of nails I spend my time making jewelry, playing with makeup, hunting for the perfect outfit, and doing hair — though honestly, I could talk about my dog for the rest of my life.

Nails have been a passion of mine for years, and these past couple of seasons have given me so many chances to practice my craft and make new friends along the way. I can't wait to turn something I love this much into a career, and I'm endlessly grateful for the support that's gotten me here. Here's to building a clientele, meeting new people, and the next chapter ahead — I can't wait to see you in my chair.
